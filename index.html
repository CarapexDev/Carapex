InfinityCoat™

Tablet coating & shelf-life barrier film

Greener, cheaper, healthier

01 • Stability & shelf life

Extend shelf life at the barrier

InfinityCoat™ forms dense, transparent films that slow oxygen-driven oxidation and moisture-driven hydrolysis — with a conservative shelf-life extension target of up to 120%.

02 • Economics & supply chain

Reduce expiry-driven write-offs

Globally, an estimated $33B of medicine expires before it can be used. By extending stability windows, InfinityCoat™ reduces waste, lowers re-manufacturing pressure, and helps manufacturers, healthcare providers, and consumers capture more value from every batch.

03 • Sustainability

Cut waste and plastic reliance

InfinityCoat™ is made from chitin nanofibers sourced from recycled organic material like crustacean shells or mushrooms. Longer shelf life can also reduce dependence on plastic blister packaging across the supply chain.

Products & Solutions

InfinityCoat™ tablet coatings engineered for barrier performance

InfinityCoat™ is a chitin nanofiber-based film coating designed to block oxygen and moisture — the two primary drivers of API degradation. We plan to sell our formulation to pharmaceutical manufacturers and CDMOs, designed to run on existing pan and fluid-bed coaters with minimal workflow disruption.

InfinityCoat™ Film Formulation

Chitin nanofiber-based tablet coating engineered for industry-leading oxygen and moisture barrier performance.

  • Compatible with standard pan and fluid-bed coaters
  • Tunable film thickness and permeability targets
  • Digestible, antimicrobial biomaterial backbone

Tech Transfer & Scale-Up Packages

Formulation handoff designed to fit manufacturer workflows — from pilot trials through commercial validation.

  • Documentation support for quality and regulatory teams

Barrier & Stability Characterization

Performance testing that quantifies oxygen/moisture transmission, film integrity, and real-world shelf-life impact.

Applications

Built for tablet manufacturers, CDMOs, and innovators

InfinityCoat™ is designed for pharmaceutical manufacturers and CDMOs looking to extend tablet shelf life, reduce expiry-driven waste, and improve sustainability — without changing coating infrastructure.

Shelf-Life Extension

Increase stability for oxygen-sensitive and moisture-sensitive tablet formulations.

Moisture & Oxygen Sensitive APIs

Slow oxidation and hydrolysis by limiting oxygen and water ingress through the coating film.

Packaging Reduction

Longer shelf life can reduce dependence on plastic blister packaging and cold-chain constraints.

Sustainable Consumer Health

Enable lower-waste tablet coatings for OTC and consumer health products using sustainably sourced chitin nanofibers.

Research & Development

Translational chitin science for tablet-coating films

Our R&D programs focus on how chitin nanofiber film structure drives oxygen/moisture barrier performance — translating directly into predictable shelf-life gains.
