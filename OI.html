Products & Solutions

Chitin systems engineered for capsule lines

Carapex delivers a family of chitin-based systems that plug directly into existing capsule, coating, and packaging lines. Each module is designed to be configurable, regulatory-ready, and built for scale.

Chitin Tumbler™ Platform

Integrated tumbling and coating system for hard capsules, pairing chitin slurries with precise controls for uniform shell coverage.

  • Retrofit-ready skids for existing lines
  • Inline coating-thickness monitoring
  • Electronic batch record integration

Chitin Coating Concentrates

Pharma-grade chitin dispersions tailored to capsule chemistry, solvent systems, and target permeability profiles.

  • Custom viscosity and solids loading
  • Support for moisture-sensitive APIs
  • Tech-transfer support from pilot to scale

Antimicrobial Surface Coatings

Chitin-based surface systems for cleanroom panels, contact points, and ancillary equipment in pharma environments.

  • Designed for high-touch facilities
  • Configurable wear and clean-down profiles
  • Compatible with existing cleaning regimes
